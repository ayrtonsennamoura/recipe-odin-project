Feijoada

Recipe:

500g black beans
- 500g pork ribs
- 500g beef chuck
- 500g pork shoulder
- 300g smoked sausage
- 2 onions, chopped
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 bay leaves
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Farofa (toasted cassava flour) and white rice, to serve
.

Instructions:

- Soak the black beans in water overnight or for at least 8 hours. Drain and rinse.
- In a large pot, cook the pork ribs, beef chuck, and pork shoulder over medium-high heat until browned on all sides. Remove from the pot and set aside.
- Add the onions to the pot and cook until translucent. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
- Return the meat to the pot and add the drained black beans, bay leaves, and enough water to cover everything. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 2-3 hours, or until the beans are tender and the meat is falling off the bone.
- Remove the meat from the pot and shred it, discarding any bones or excess fat.
- Cut the smoked sausage into slices and add it to the pot, along with the shredded meat. Cook for another 20 minutes.
- Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
- Serve the feijoada hot with farofa and white rice on the side.
