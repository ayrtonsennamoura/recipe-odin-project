Arroz Carreteiro

Recipe:

- 2 cups of rice - 500g of beef jerky, chopped into small pieces;
- 1 large onion, chopped;
- 3 cloves of garlic, minced;
- 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil;
- 2 teaspoons of salt;
- 1 teaspoon of black pepper;
- 1 teaspoon of cumin;
- 1 liter of water.

Instructions:

- In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and saut√© until the onion is translucent.
- Add the beef jerky to the pot, and stir until it is lightly browned.
- Add the rice to the pot, and stir to combine with the beef jerky and onion mixture.
- Add the salt, black pepper, and cumin to the pot, and stir to combine.
- Add the water to the pot, and stir gently to combine. Bring the mixture to a boil over high heat.
- Once the mixture comes to a boil, reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with a lid. Simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is fully cooked and has absorbed all of the liquid.
- Once the rice is fully cooked, remove the pot from the heat and let it sit, covered, for about 5 minutes.
- Remove the lid from the pot, and fluff the rice with a fork. Serve hot.
Enjoy your "arroz carreteiro"!
