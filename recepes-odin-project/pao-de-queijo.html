Pão de Queijo

Recipe:

500g black beans;
- 500g of sweet tapioca starch ;
- 250ml of milk;
- 125ml of oil;
- 2 eggs;
- 1 teaspoon of salt;
- 250g of grated cured minas cheese.

Instructions:

- Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
- In a saucepan, mix the milk and oil and heat until boiling.
- In a bowl, place the tapioca starch and add the hot mixture. Mix well.
- Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
- Add the salt and grated cheese and mix until a homogeneous dough is formed.
- Shape the cheese bread into small balls and place them on a greased baking sheet.
- Bake for about 25 minutes or until golden brown.
